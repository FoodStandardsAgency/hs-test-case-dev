About the Cover:
An oligosaccharide (H2N2F2) was found to be involved in specific recognition and binding of the human norovirus on the leaf of romaine lettuce. This oligosaccharide consists of two hexoses, two N-acetylhexosamines and two fucoses. Structural formulas: blue square, N-acetylglucosamine; yellow square, N-acetylgalactosamine; yellow circle, galactose; red triangles, fucose.
